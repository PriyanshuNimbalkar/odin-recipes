Gulwani

Gulwani (sometimes called "Gulavani" or "Gulavani Shev") is a traditional Maharashtrian sweet snack made from wheat flour and jaggery, shaped into thin sticks or spirals and deep-fried. It is enjoyed as a crunchy, sweet treat.

Ingredients to make

  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup jaggery
  • Water as needed
  • 1 tablespoon ghee or oil (for dough)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps to make

  1. Melt jaggery in a little water to make a syrup. Let it cool.
  2. Mix wheat flour, cardamom powder, and ghee/oil.
  3. Add jaggery syrup gradually to form a soft dough.
  4. Shape dough into thin sticks or spirals using a sev press or by hand.
  5. Optional: Sun-dry for a few hours for extra crispiness.
  6. Deep fry in hot oil until golden and crisp.
  7. Drain on paper towels and cool before storing.
  8. Enjoy as a sweet, crunchy snack.
